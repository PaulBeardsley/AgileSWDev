Match the term with its definition.

Definitions

1. This is a daily 15-minute meeting used to optimise communication across the team.

2. An opportunity for the scrum team to inspect itself and create a plan for improvements to be made during the next sprint.

3. This meeting is held at the beginning of the sprint. The meeting will usually take the full day for a two-week sprint.

4. Held at the end of the sprint to inspect the work done and adapt the product backlog.

Terms

a. Sprint planning meeting
b. Daily Stand up
c. Sprint Review Meeting
d. The sprint retrospective meeting

Please place the Sprint ceremonies in the correct order that they occur in a sprint (starting with the earliest).
