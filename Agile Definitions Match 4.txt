Definitions

1. Manages projects and will overview expenses.

2. A multi-disciplinary team usually consists of software architects, designers, programmers, and testers.

3. Supports the Product Owner by gathering requirements and providing guidance on what to build.

4. Anyone with an interest in or an influence on the product.

5. Helps those outside the Scrum team understand which interactions are beneficial.

6. The sole person responsible for managing the product backlog (i.e., the list of features to be worked on).

b. Product Owner
